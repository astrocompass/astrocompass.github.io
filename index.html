About AstroCompass

As the AstroCompass team, we prioritize the principle of sustainability in space exploration. Our project aims to reprocess and reuse space debris and waste that may accumulate on the surface of Mars. Specifically, we focus on recycling metal-based waste, transforming it into reusable materials that can support future human settlements. In doing so, we strive to reduce resource consumption and build a sustainable infrastructure for long-term missions on Mars.
